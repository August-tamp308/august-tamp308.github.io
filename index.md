## 🛠️ Core Features

This software includes tools designed for high-end visual effects and post-production tasks.

*   Spline Tools: Place points to create shapes around objects. You use these shapes to cut out elements frame by frame.
*   Point Tracking: The software follows movement in your video. It moves your shapes automatically to match moving parts of your footage.
*   Matte Extraction: You can blend edges soft or hard to make your cutouts look natural against new backgrounds.
*   Color Correction: Adjust colors inside your masked areas to match the environment.
*   Paint Tools: Remove unwanted wires, film dirt, or glitches from your frames.

## 📝 Usage Guidelines

Work in a logical order to save time during your rotoscoping workflow.

Identify the movement in your video first. Use keyframes at the start and end of a movement. The software calculates the path between these points. You only need to adjust the middle points if the path drifts. Save your work often by pressing Ctrl and S. Export your finished work as a sequence for use in editing programs.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

Follow these steps if you experience issues during use.

*   The application does not start: Check if you have the latest drivers for your graphics card. Update your drivers through your hardware manufacturer website.
*   The video does not play smooth: Lower the preview resolution in the viewer settings. This reduces the strain on your processor during playback.
*   The software crashes during render: Clear your cache in the Preferences menu. Large projects often fill your temporary storage space completely.
*   The interface looks blurry: Right-click the application shortcut, select Properties, and click Change high DPI settings. Check the override box and select System.
